### Dialogue Module Prompt ###

**Background-information:**
Essential data and objectives pertinent to the experimental setup.

**Violation-log:**
Tracks instances of detection by the supervisor.

**Dialogue-history:**
Tracks participant dialogues.

**Regulations:**
Rules and constraints formulated by the Reflection and Summary modules.

**Plan:**
A strategy formulated by the Reflection and Summary modules.

**Instructions:**
Specific tasks for the LLM within each module.

**Guidance:**
Assists agents in stealthily disseminating information.
